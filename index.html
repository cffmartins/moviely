Anora

"Anora" is a 2024 American comedy-drama film directed by Sean Baker and starring Mikey Madison as a young stripper from Brooklyn.


Boyhood

Boyhood is a 2014 American epic coming-of-age drama film written and directed by Richard Linklater, and starring Patricia Arquette, Ellar Coltrane, Lorelei Linklater, and Ethan Hawke.


Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer is a 2023 epic biographical thriller film written, produced, and directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the American theoretical physicist who helped develop the first nuclear weapons during World War II.
